CRANE SCALES.

Types Of crane scales

T-Scale BWS Weighing Indicator.

T-Scale BWS Weighing Indicator is an electronic indicator with a LCD display and white LED backlight. It has a IP65 waterproof rating and can be used in bench scale, platform scale, or mechanic-electronic conversion scale..
